Starverse

Launch Starverse

Starverse is a responsive 3D interactive stargazing tour built to map thousands of stars using local astronomical datasets. Functioning completely offline, Starverse coordinates stellar vectors (X, Y, Z coordinates), color temperatures (CI metrics), and brightness magnitudes visually. Toggling the AR Orientation mode allows users to track stars in real-time space surrounding their device. Experience direct, secure, and zero-latency cosmos exploration on any browser.

Movie Story

Launch Movie Story

MovieStory is a modern, 100% free Audio Movie Podcast and Discovery Platform. It offers an integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS) audio player to listen to movie synopses and in-depth spoilers in 18 languages—perfect for listening on the go. Powered by TMDB, it provides up-to-date casting, OTT streaming info, and auto-playlist modes. Built as a sleek PWA with a premium dark-mode UI, it requires no login and is fully optimized for rich social sharing.
