Professional Summary

Festival-recognized Director of Photography with over 14 years of experience across feature films and OTT productions in multiple Indian languages. Known for crafting emotionally grounded visual narratives supported by strong technical expertise in digital color science, HDR mastering, and multi-format delivery workflows. His work bridges emotional realism with technical precision, combining performance-driven visual storytelling with advanced knowledge of HDR imaging and wide-gamut color pipelines.

As Director of Photography for Kaadhal Enbathu Pothu Udamai (2025), his cinematography contributed to the film’s selection at the 54th International Film Festival of India (Indian Panorama), Pune International Film Festival, Habitat International Film Festival, and Chennai International Film Festival, where it also received major awards. Comfortable working across both film and digital ecosystems, he has extensive experience with theatrical exhibition standards and OTT HDR workflows, ensuring visual consistency from on-set capture to final display.

Visual Approach

"I approach cinematography as emotional architecture. Light is not used merely to illuminate, but to shape psychological space. My visual language is grounded in realism yet responsive to narrative tension, balancing organic textures with structured composition. I believe color is memory, contrast is conflict, and shadow is subtext. Whether working within controlled LED volume environments or natural night exteriors, I design frames that serve performance first, allowing technology to support storytelling rather than dominate it."
